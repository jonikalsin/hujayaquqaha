Is Mike from American Pickers married?

Mike Wolfe married Jodi Faeth on September 8, 2012.

Their daughter, Charlie, was born a few months prior in January of the same year.

In November of 2020, Jodi filed for divorce from Mike citing irreconcilable differences.

At the time, she listed June of 2020 has their date of separation.

In the divorce settlement filed with the court and obtained by The Sun, Mike was ordered to pay Jodi around $634,000 in alimony.

Their divorce was officially finalized in December of 2021.

Is Mike Wolfe dating anybody?

Following his split from Jodi Faeth, Mike Wolfe began dating model and reality-television personality Leticia Cline.

Leticia was the former interviewer for TNA Wrestling, and has appeared in magazines like Maxim and Playboy.

She joined the cast of the hit reality show Beauty and the Geek in 2008 for the fifth and final season of the series.

What is American Pickers?

American Pickers first premiered on January 18, 2010.

The show follows antique and collectible picker Mike Wolfe, who travels across the United States in search of various items for resale.

Frank Fritz, his former co-star and partner, is no longer a member of the show.

Wolfe’s personal business, Antique Archaeology, runs out of both Iowa and Tennessee.

A total of 23 seasons of the show have released over the last 12 years.

What is Mike Wolfe’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Mike Wolfe has an estimated net worth of around $7million.

Apart from his role as the star of American Pickers, Wolfe is also credited as a show creator and executive producer.

He signed an endorsement deal with Indian Motorcycles in 2012, and guest-starred in a 2018 episode of NCIS.

Wolfe has also published two books: American Pickers Guide to Picking (2011); and Kid Pickers: How to Turn Junk into Treasure (2013).
