LHHATL Mimi Faust Relationships And Dating History

  • Ty Long

Mimi Faust’s last known girlfriend/fiancee is Ty Young.

The two have had a tumultuous relationship over the past few years. Starting their love story in 2016, the couple went on to get engaged in 2020. However, less than a year after Ty proposed, the couple called off their engagement.

According to Mimi, they ultimately wanted “different” things out of life. And when Ty suddenly proposed she felt pressured. “I had expressed this to Ty, but Ty is a born athlete, she’s a natural-born winner, she’s just used to getting what she wants. And so that is what she wanted and she pressed forward with what she wanted, regardless of anybody else saying how they felt about it,” she explained.

But surprisingly, they reconciled and gave fans the good news via IG live. So, fast-forward to January 2022,  the couple got engaged again and it was Ty again who popped the question. Unfortunately, what’s never meant to happen will never happen. In March 2022, a viral video circulated showing Ty Young cozying up to another woman. Not soon after Ty announced that she was a single woman.

Just a few days before Ty announced she was single, Mimi had made a WCW post on Instagram tagging Ty.
