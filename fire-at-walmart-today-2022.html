Publish date: 2024-08-27

Fire at Walmart today 2022 – EMERGENCY crews responded to the scene of a fire at a Walmart Monday in Nevada.

The fire was reported around 4pm at 3075 E Tropicana Avenue.

The flames resulted in a large cloud of smoke. The fire reportedly occurred on the east side of the valley.

8 News Now reports that the fire involved tractor-trailers and cardboard boxes behind the building.

No injuries have been reported at this time. An evacuation of the store is currently underway.

Videos on social media showed shoppers fleeing from the building.
