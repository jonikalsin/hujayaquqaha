The Kinjaz (Dance Crew) Wiki - Stark Times

Publish date: 2024-05-12

The Kinjaz is a LA, California, USA-based dance group, crew, fashion brand, and Media team. It was found by a popular dance and choreographer named Anthony Lee

along with his friend and dancer Mike Song in 2010. Presently, the group is known as the one of leading dance crews in the world. 

Table of Contents

Dance Competitions

The Kinjaz has performed in multiple dance competitions around the world including in the 8th season of America’s Best Dance Crew, season 1 of World of Dance, and many others. 

Crew Members

Presently, there are six popular dancers in the group and experts of different dancing styles named Anthony Lee, Mike Song, Keone Madrid, Pat Cruz, Ben Chung, and Lawrence Devera. Furthermore, there are many guest dancers as well.

Different Ventures

The group trains at its own studio named Kinjaz Dojo located in Los Angeles, California. Besides this, Kinjaz has an apparel brand Kin Aesthetik and a snack brand named KinjaBang Noodles.

Youtube Channel

The Kinjaz has a self-titled Youtube channel with more than 1.13m subscribers as of now. The group mostly shares dancing and choreography videos on the channel. In 2012, the channel was created and uploaded a debut video titled “KINJAZ : A DANCE ANIME” which has 83k views as of now. The most viewed video on the channel is “Kendrick Lamar “Humble” | Choreography by The Kinjaz” which has more than 9.3m views as of now.
