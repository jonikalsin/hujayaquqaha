Solange Knowles Nip Slip — Wardrobe Malfunction On Wedding Day

Solange married her fiancé Alan Ferguson, 51, in a glorious wedding in New Orleans on Nov. 16. However, there was one little mishap!

During a wedding after-party in New Orleans’ French Quarter, Solange had an accidental nip slip! As she danced at the Mardi Gras-style parade, she suffered a wardrobe malfunction.

Unfortunately, the neckline on her gorgeous white jumpsuit made her susceptible to the malfunction. Poor Solange.

However, the slight fashion mishap could have been a lot worse on her wedding day. 

Solange Knowles Marries Alan Ferguson: All The Details On The Wedding

Solange’s jumpsuit was the third outfit she wore on her wedding day! She arrived to the Marigny Opera House on Nov. 16 in a gorgeous white pantsuit, which also had a plunging neckline.

For her actual nuptials, Solange wore a chic white gown with a cape. The dress was designed by Humberto Leon. She looked so lovely and so beautiful!

Sister Beyonce, 33, Jay Z, 44, and Blue Ivy, 2, were also on hand for the ceremony. Beyonce and Blue donned pretty white dresses, while Jay wore a white suit.
